Ode to Mr. G

In a world where shadows often sway, There walks a man who lights the way, With wisdom deep and heart so true, We sing our praise for Mr. G—it’s you. Your voice, a compass in the storm, A steady hand, a heart so warm. You teach us how to rise, to strive, In every lesson, dreams revive. Though accolades may fill the air, You wear them with a humble glare, For lives you’ve shaped, like rivers flow, Their currents bloom where you’ve sown growth. An oak in winds, unyielding, strong, You right the weak and right the wrong. Your patience bends like endless streams, Nurturing seeds of buried dreams. In every soul you’ve touched with grace, The world finds a brighter place. A ripple in the timeless sea, Your legacy lives endlessly. So here’s to you, our steadfast star, Though near or distant, where you are, Your light shines on, it will not dim— Thank you, Mr. G, for the fire within. For footprints left in sands of time, In hearts, in minds, in rhythm, rhyme, The heavens scrawl your name in praise: A life well-lived, a beacon’s blaze.
